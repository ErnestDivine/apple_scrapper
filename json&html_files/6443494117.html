    Description

    Unlock the science behind your beauty routine with Stilla.


    Scan any product to instantly reveal what's in it, how it might benefit your skin, and any potential irritants. Stilla empowers informed choices, whether you're a skincare beginner or a pro.


    With Stilla, you can:


    ▸ Scan barcodes or search our extensive product database to find products that address your specific skin type and concerns.

    ▸ Discover new favorites and trending ingredients with our curated collections.

    ▸ Analyze ingredients directly from labels or pasted text. Stilla will break down confusing ingredient lists and explain what each ingredient brings to the overall product formula.

    ▸ Get personalized recommendations based on your unique goals, and optimize your routine according to your personal goals.

    ▸ Track problematic ingredients to avoid them (you choose which ones, we don't decide this for you!)

    ▸ Track expiration dates: Stilla takes into account each product's individual Period-After-Opening.

    ▸ Create custom routines and follow them with Live Activities.

    ▸ Sync all your data across devices with iCloud.


    More ways Stilla helps:


    • Organize your skincare collection: Bookmark your favorite products and keep track of everything in one place.

    • Get exclusive insights: See star ingredients and Tracked Ingredient warnings.

    • Verify marketing claims: Don't take promises at face value, let Stilla do the research.

    • Siri & Shortcuts: Start routines and access features with voice commands.

    • Customize your app: Choose an icon that matches your style.


    Why choose Stilla?


    › Shop smarter:

    Scan labels while shopping for beauty products to find the perfect match for your skin.


    › Revamp your routine:

    Optimize your current skincare regimen for better results.


    › Avoid irritants:

    Identify common allergens and irritants that might cause reactions.


    › Clean beauty made easy:

    Track custom ingredients and find products that align with your values.


    › Stay informed:

    Be notified of product reformulations and get the latest ingredient info.


    › Build a dream routine:

    See how well a new product will fit into your existing routine.


    › Learn as you go:

    Discover the science behind popular ingredients and their benefits.



    Stilla is an independent app created by just two people dedicated to making skincare information transparent and accessible. Our algorithms are not biased towards any brand and rely solely on scientific evidence behind each ingredient.


    We care about your privacy: we do not use any trackers from Meta, Google, or any other advertising platform.

    Get ready for an upgraded skincare experience with Stilla’s latest update.


    This update expands the app’s capabilities, enabling you to complete familiar actions more efficiently, discover new features, and accomplish more with Stilla.


    Here’s what’s new:


    Controls

    Quickly access actions like starting a routine, scanning ingredients, or searching products from the Control Center or Lock Screen.


    Shortcuts & Siri Integration

    Stilla is now more convenient than ever! Use new shortcuts to decode ingredients or start barcode scanning instantly. Available via Siri, Spotlight, the Action Button, and more.


    Updated Live Activities

    You can now start a saved routine through a Control, a Shortcut, or a Widget. Follow your routines even more effortlessly with Live Activities on Apple Watch.
