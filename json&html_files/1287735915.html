    Less waiting!

    I prefer to see my doctor in person; however, this app allows you to check-in and then instead of waiting, it sends you a text letting you know when your doctor is ready. So you can work or do other things instead of sitting in a waiting room or exam room. Plus, the doctors are more adamant about staying on schedule. And it holds all your insurance and pharmacy info. Highly recommend if short on time or just need a referral or have something that doesn’t require a physical exam.

    Teacup-Tempest , 12/03/2020

    Serious voiceover Accessibility Issues

    To say that I am disappointed in the level of inaccessibility I found in this app would be an understatement. From the signup screen alone it was clear that blind users were not considered.

    Before text is entered into the fields voiceover reads the labels, but as soon as text is entered voiceover does not read it or the accompanying label. The gender selection options, the button to agree to the terms of service and the button to register are not clickable.

    There are other problems once an account has been created, and I haven’t even attempted the video appointment yet. This isn’t a silly game, this is a telehealth app in the middle of a pandemic. These access issues need to be fixed.
