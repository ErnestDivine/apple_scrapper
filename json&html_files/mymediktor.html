    Description

    Solve your health questions from the first symptom.


    Check your symptoms for FREE.


    The symptom checker allows you to input how you feel with your own words. Its artificial intelligence picks the questions a professional would ask to get to a list of possible conditions and a recommendation.


    See the description of diseases in our dictionary.


    Please do not hesitate to send us your suggestions or comments: info@mediktor.com


    Specs:


    Artificial intelligence and natural language interpretation.

    Compatible with the Apple Health App for the integration of vital signs.
