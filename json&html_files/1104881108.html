    Description

    HealthJoy is a first-of-its-kind employee benefits experience platform. We make understanding and using your company’s health benefits simple, quick and easy. With your membership, you’ll get unlimited access to:


    • Healthcare concierges that make appointments, answer questions and more


    • Online medical consultations with prescription writing - 24/7


    • A personalized benefits wallet will your company’s benefit cards


    • Get recommendations for an in-network local doctor or facility


    • We can explain and advocate on your behalf for medical bills


    • Discover savings for your prescriptions


    NOTE: You must have a company-sponsored membership to use HealthJoy.

    Efficient and Recommended

    My mother told me about this app for our health insurance plan and I had never used it until recently. I had noticed I was being overcharged for copays at my local doctors office and was on hold for half an hour before remembering I had this app! I submitted some pictures of my bills and within a few days they had accessed my claims, gotten in touch with the office billing department for me, and have notified me of the errors made. They got me a refund for everything I was overcharged for and it took me no longer than 5 minutes to provide the info they needed without making any calls in the process. They have been efficient in helping me find providers in my network and answering any and all questions I’ve had. I really recommend this app and I believe it will save lots of people time and unnecessary frustration/confusion!!
